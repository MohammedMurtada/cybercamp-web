PANCAKE

Pancake is a dessert that is primarily made up of egg,flour and milk.Sometimes fruits like strawberry or raspberry are added to it alongside honey They are several steps when it comes to the making of pancake which include:

  • Grab a griddle or large nonstick skillet
  • Preheat and lightly grease your pan
  • Use a simple ratio to make batter
  • Dont overbeat the batter
  • Cook each pancake until bubbles form on top
  • Add toppings before you flip
  • Flip and let cook for another minute
